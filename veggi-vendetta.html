Gameplay

Veggi Vendetta is a 2-4 player local multiplayer game. The players take the role of different vegetables. The goal of every vegetable is to jump into the cooking pot in the middle of the map in order to be cooked! However, before jumping in, the players have to collect points by eating the other vegetables. This is achieved by dashing into another player. Once a player has earned a point they can jump into the pot to save their score. If they get eaten they lose all their unsaved points. A player can hold up to 5 points at a time. The game is over once the timer runs out. The player with the most saved points wins.

Development

Veggi Vendetta was my first real project made with Unity. It was developed during a one weekend long game jam. I worked in a rather large team of 8 people. The team consisted of 3 artists and 5 programmers. I was responsible for UI programming and created the main menu, the character selection, and the end screen.
